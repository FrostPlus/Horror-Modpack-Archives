Horror Modpack

About This Modpack

This horror-themed Minecraft modpack draws heavy inspiration from games like Darkwood. Expect tension, survival mechanics, and immersive darkness.

Features

  • Custom Minecraft menu with horror styling
  • Ambient sounds and dynamic lighting
  • Slow, stalking cave dwellers
  • Realistic torch lighting behavior
